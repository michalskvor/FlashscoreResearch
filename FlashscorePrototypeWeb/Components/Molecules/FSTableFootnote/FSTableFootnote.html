Long Text (Wrapping)

Expected goals (xG): is a statistical measure of the quality of chances created and chances conceded. It is calculated based on several variables such as shot angle, distance from goal, body part used, type of pass and type of attack. The xG of a single shot ranges from 0 to 1 with 1 being the highest chance of scoring.
